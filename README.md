# TAROT ORACLE 🔮
An AI-powered tarot divination website.

You can randomly choose your tarot spreads and draw tarot cards, enter your own questions, and receive personalized tarot interpretations.
## Project Intro

From concept design, page layout and feature implementation to deployment on GitHub and Vercel, the whole project was developed with the assistance of Claude Code.

Modern people are often trapped by life pressure, emotional anxiety and confusion about the future. 

That’s why the idea of creating a tarot website deeply resonated with me.

Last not the least, this is my first time to build a AI product by vibe coding.😄
Creating and building my own project from 0 to 1 brings me a strong sense of achievement，and I will continue to optimize and update the project.

Your opinions and suggestions are highly welcome! Hope you enjoy it :)
